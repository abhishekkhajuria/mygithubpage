The Nominees



After the much contested primaries in the first half of this year, We are finally left with these candidates from each of the major parties: Donald Trump (Republican) and Hillary Clinton (Democrat).






Insights, As they happen



We monitor Twitter for mentions of the Democratic and Republican Presidential Nominees, analyze the sentiment of the content and display it graphically. Both the data and tweets update in real time so you can track the conversation as it happens!


















So what does twitter say?















SPOT ON!




















      A word of caution:

  • The sample size may be too small to provide insights into the collective opinion.

  • The opinion of the Twitterati may not reflect the actual popularity or the election result.

  • The sentiment algorithm may not be reliably accurate.
